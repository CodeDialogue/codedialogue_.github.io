BASKETBALL ABBREVATIONS

PTS - points scored by player or team
AST - assists by player or team
REB - rebounds by player or team
STL - steals by player or team
BLK - blocks by player or team
TOV - turnovers by player or team
FGA - field goals attempted by player or team
FGM - field goals made by player or team
3PA - three point attempts by player or team
3PM - three point made by player or team
hAST% - percentage of a player's possessions that end in an assist
IN - minutes and seconds the player entered the game, format 3:40
SCORE IN - score when player entered the game, format 34-43
FTA - free throws attempted by player or team
FTM - free throws made by player or team
2PA - two point attempts by player or team
2PM - two point made by player or team
2P% - two point percentage by player or team
3P% - three point percentage by player or team
eFG% - effective field goal percentage by player or team
TS% - true shooting percentage by player or team
PER - player efficiency rating
GmSc - game score
TOV% - percentage of a player's possessions that end in a turnover
OUT - minutes and seconds the player left the game, format 2:31
SCORE OUT - score when player left the game, format 48-51

SOCCER ABBREVATIONS

GA - number of goals scored against a team or goalkeeper during a specific period of time
GAA - number of goals the goalie allows for each unit of regulation game time
SH% - percentage of shots that player makes
AST - assists by player or team
MP - minutes played
SV% - percentage of shots that keeper saves
PTS - points gained by player by scoring or assisting
